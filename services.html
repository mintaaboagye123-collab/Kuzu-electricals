OUR SERVICES

Electrical Installation

Wiring of new houses, shops, schools, and offices. Installing sockets, switches, light fittings, ceiling fans, and circuit breakers. Setting up main distribution boards and meter connections.

Electrical Maintenance and Repair

Fixing faulty wiring, burnt sockets, or broken light fittings. Troubleshooting electrical faults such as power surges or tripped breakers. Replacing old or unsafe wiring to meet safety standards.Installing indoor and outdoor lighting (e.g., streetlights, security lights). Designing energy-efficient lighting systems using LED bulbs. Setting up backup power systems like generators or inverters..

Industrial and Commercial Services

CInstalling three-phase systems and heavy-duty machines. Maintaining motors, control panels, and factory electrical systems. Setting up automated systems and programmable controllers..

Installation of Water Pumps Maintenance and Repair

Electricians connect the pump motor to the power supply safely. They install control switches, starter panels, and circuit breakers. They ensure correct wiring and earthing to prevent electric shocks.Checking and replacing burnt motors, faulty capacitors, or damaged cables. Diagnosing problems like the pump not starting or tripping the circuit. Servicing motors to improve performance and reduce power consumption..
